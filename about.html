About Us

Our Team

Infection Control Results has brought together a skilled and experienced team of professionals both within and
outside of the medical field.

Our owner/president, Luci Perri, has more than 20 years of experience in infection prevention and control. She has earned a
master's degree in community health nursing, and public health from The University of South Carolina and is Board certified
in infection prevention and control. Perri served in the United States Air Force for 20 years, retiring in 2007. As a result, she is
familiar with both military and civilian healthcare settings and protocols.

I have known Luci for over five years as an Infection Prevention colleague," says Shelby Lassiter, Clinical Content Development Lead at Heath Research and Educational Trust, American Hospital Association. "She is very knowledgeable and has a great deal of excellent clinical experience in infection prevention. She worked with me at the NC Quality Center as a Team Coach and worked with teams to facilitate improvement in HAIs. She is a fantastic team player and is very personable."
What Makes Us Unique?

Infection Control Results provides infection prevention and control services to acute care, long-term care, rehab facilities, dialysis centers
and ambulatory settings, including private medical offices, dental offices, and ambulatory surgery centers.

Why Spend The Extra Time When
We Have the Skills?

We understand that you don't have extra time to create, implement and monitor an infection prevention and control program. That's where we can help. There's no reason to take time away from your patients when we have proven systems that work to control healthcare-associated infections (HAIs), such as pneumonia, blood infections, and UTIs.

Infection Control Results will visit your facility and do a thorough on-site assessment of your office or clinic's procedures for preventing HAIs, or we can simply consult over the phone. Then, we'll meet to decide on a plan of action to improve or replace your existing procedures. Our plan will show you how to educate your staff and conduct continuing education, monitoring and periodic reviews and reassessments to see how your facility is faring. Of course, our staff will be available to you throughout the process to answer questions and provide support.
